Telegram Bot (free, uses Telegram app)
If you use Telegram, you can create a bot that sends messages directly to your Telegram account.

Steps:

Create a bot: talk to @BotFather on Telegram, send /newbot, get a token.

Find your chat ID: send a message to your bot, then visit https://api.telegram.org/bot<YOUR_TOKEN>/getUpdates to see your chat ID.
